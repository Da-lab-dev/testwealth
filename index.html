Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This site is made to help Nigerian law students access study materials, case summaries, and legal documents easily in one place.

Are all the resources free to download?

Yes, most materials are free. Some premium resources may require a small fee to support website maintenance.

Can I contribute my own notes or materials?

Absolutely. You can submit your lecture notes, case summaries, or past questions through the “Contact” or “Upload” page

Do these materials cover all Nigerian universities?

Yes. Most topics are based on the standard Nigerian law curriculum used across universities and law schools.

How can I prepare for law exams using these resources?

Start by reading lecture notes, then go through study guides and statutes and Acts. Practice writing answers like you would in the exam hall.

How do I download materials from the site?

Just go to the Resources page, pick the topic you want, and click “Download.” Files are usually in PDF format.
